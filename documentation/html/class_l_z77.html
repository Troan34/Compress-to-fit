void LZ77< DataType >::compress ( CodecInterface< Sym, LZ77_Token > & interface)

Compress the data up to max_index.

Parameters
out_streamWhere the output will go
max_indexThe last index to get compressed (included)
Warning
#out_stream will be cleared and overwritten

This function shall be used as a compression "in steps". Its use is to stop the #out_stream from being too big. The function shall not strictly stop at #max_index, due to the nature of the LZ77 algorithm.
